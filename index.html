About

My name is Muhamad Ali Imron, I am a Vocational High School student majoring in Computer and Network Engineering at SMKN 3 Bogor City. I am very interested in computer related matters. Sometimes I self-taught on the internet about the world of computers and networks.

When I was in elementary school, I attended SDN Gondang Rejo in Boyolali district, Central Java from grade 1 to grade 4. Then I moved to SDN Ponduk Rumput in Bogor City. After I graduated from SD I decided to enter SMPN 2 Bogor City. Three years passed. I graduated from junior high school, then I decided to enter my current school, namely SMKN 3 Bogor City.
